Astral Fish ringtones are short songs, discreet alerts, and annoying alarms made from scratch.

When I first bought an iPhone I wanted sounds that were custom built to be ringtones from the start and with 25 years of making music behind me it wasn’t long before I started making my own.

I use my iPad and iPhone to make each ringtone and artwork that goes with it. This allows me to make ringtones that are tested right on the speaker you’ll hear them from.

Each purchase includes a ringtone you can drag directly into iTunes, an mp3 version for non-Apple devices, and the artwork I created specifically for the ringtone.
